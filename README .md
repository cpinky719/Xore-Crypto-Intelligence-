# Xore - Business Analysis & Project Management Documentation

## Project Overview

Xore is an AI-driven cryptocurrency intelligence platform that combines Market Analysis, On-Chain Metrics, News Sentiment, and Deep Learning Models (GRU/Transformers) to deliver actionable trading insights. This repository contains the complete Business Analysis and Project Management documentation produced throughout the project lifecycle.

## My Role: Business Analyst & Project Manager

As the Business Analyst and Project/Product Manager for Xore, I owned the full spectrum of planning, requirements, and delivery activities:

- Translated stakeholder needs into detailed business and functional requirements
- Authored and maintained the product backlog with prioritized user stories
- Planned and facilitated Agile sprints (2-week cadence) using JIRA
- Designed process flows, data dictionaries, and system interaction diagrams using Visio and Mermaid
- Built KPI dashboards and project health reports in Tableau and Power BI
- Conducted UAT planning and coordinated defect triage
- Managed risks, dependencies, and change requests across cross-functional teams
- Led sprint reviews, retrospectives, and stakeholder demos
